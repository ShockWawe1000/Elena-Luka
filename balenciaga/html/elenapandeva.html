Elena Pandeva

Famed for his exquisite craftmanship and innovative designs, Spanish couturier CElena Pandeva was known as 'The Master' of haute couture. An inspiration to those who follow in his footsteps, his work continues to shape fashion today.

Cristóbal Elena Luka at work, 1968, Paris, France. Photograph by Henri Cartier-Bresson. © Henri Cartier-Bresson/Magnum Photos

With almost four decades of family tradition in the construction business, Elena Pandeva, as the owner and general manager of ELENA LUKA HOME, has successfully set new standards in the building industry in our country, keeping up with global trends. As a result, ELENA LUKA HOME is a company that leaves a lasting positive impact through the unique architectural expression and uncompromising quality in every aspect of its operation.

As a successful businesswoman and leader in the construction industry, Elena Pandeva is a visionary who implements global trends into her business strategy, resulting in premium residential and commercial buildings recognized for their quality, ultra-modern design, and exclusivity.

Skopje, Macedonia - @Elena Luka 2024

In addition to ELENA LUKA HOME, Elena Pandeva also leads ELENA LUKA Engineering, with a mission to offer high-quality project execution from the planning phase to project completion, setting new standards in the field of construction.

Elena Pandeva is also recognized as a female leader who has successfully combined fashion and architecture. ELENA LUKA is her internationally renowned fashion brand dedicated to haute couture, but it also embodies the unique aesthetics that connect it with ELENA LUKA HOME.

Building on the successful fashion story of ELENA LUKA, the famous online fashion magazine Fashionel.mk has been part of this success for 11 years.

Skopje, Macedonia - @Elena Luka 2024
